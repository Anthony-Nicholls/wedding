Catering

A cash bar will be available all day (contactless payments or cash are accepted).

Snacks and a welcome drink will be provided after the ceremony. We will be having a sit down meal at 3:30pm and evening food at 8:00pm.

If you bring any snacks with you or buy them from the cafe, we please ask that these do not contain nuts due to one of our guests having a serious nut allergy.

If you have any dietary requirements, please detail these on the RSVP.
