Our story

We met on Christmas Eve 2007 at MudClub in Bognor Regis through a mutual friend, Jasmine. Just six months later, we moved in together in sunny Bournemouth, where we were both studied at university.

During our time at Bournemouth University, we welcomed our wonderful son, Jacob, into the world. After graduating, Anthony found a job in Oxfordshire, where we’ve happily lived ever since.

During a trip to London in 2021, Anthony (finally) proposed to Cheryl at the top of The O2 and, as you’ve probably guessed by now, she said yes!

Before planning our wedding, we decided to focus on buying our first home together, and in 2023 we became the proud owners of our home in the beautiful town of Chipping Norton (yes, right near Diddly Squat Farm!).

We really hope you’ll be able to join us on our special day and experience a little of the magic of Oxfordshire and the Cotswolds, which have been our home for the past 13 years.

Venue

Cogges Manor Farm is a historic manor house and farm set within seventeen acres of beautiful open space, including an adventure playground, orchard picnic areas, a stunning walled garden, a café serving homemade food and tasty treats, and a shop selling gifts, crafts, and farm produce.

When we first moved to Oxfordshire, Cogges Manor Farm quickly became one of our favourite places to visit. A wonderful day out for a young family getting to know a new county. So when we discovered it was also licensed for wedding ceremonies, we knew it would be the perfect place for us to celebrate our special day.

Even if you’ve never visited Cogges Manor Farm before, you may still recognise it from the ITV drama Downton Abbey, where it featured as the fictional Yew Tree Farm.

You can learn more about Cogges Manor Farm and the 1'000 year history of the manor house on their website.
